I Heart Teenok Party 2011

Featured on CharityHappenings – http://charityhappenings.org

On Thursday, June 23rd, The Teenok Heart Foundation hosted its 2nd annual "I Heart Teenok Party." The evening included a double blind wine-tasting challenge & lesson led by Master Sommelier Laura Maniec, live music by acclaimed cellists from the North Sky Ensemble, and a raffle with great prizes. Guests enjoyed a Pinkberry station where they were able to create their own desserts. The event was held at Astor Center in NYC and all funds raised went to support the Teenok Heart Foundation and pediatric cardiology.

I Heart Teenok Party 2010

Featured on CharityHappenings – http://charityhappenings.org

The I Heart Teenok Party/Teenok-A-Palooza took place on Thursday April 15th, 2010. Young philanthropists came out to the exclusive first annual event held at the 5th Avenue Club, an chic private lounge in midtown Manhattan, to support the Teenok Heart Foundation and pediatric cardiology.
