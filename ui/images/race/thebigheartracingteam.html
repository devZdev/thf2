We welcome athletic enthusiasts of all ages from around the world to join the Big Heart Racing Team and raise money to support our important cause.
THF?s Big Heart Racing Team is a group open to individuals looking to raise awareness and funds for THF in various athletic endurance events. Teams have been represented in marathons, triathlons, Ironman 70.3, and bike races across the Northeast.
THF has been a charity partner in major venues such as the NYC ING Marathon, NYC Half Marathon, TD 5 Boro Bike Tour, and the NYC Triathlon.
Check back soon for other venues, as we are constantly adding events.
